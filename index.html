Who I Am

I'm That (Phukao) Prommolmard, a Computer Science and Psychology student at Grinnell College ('24), passionate about cognitive psychology research. At present, I contribute to the vibrant student music scene at Freesound. Off-duty, I'm either composing music or exploring culinary arts.

What I Do

My technical toolkit includes Python, Java, JavaScript, C, and C++, complemented by fluency in Thai and English, conversational Danish, and literacy in German and Russian. Music is my universal language, as I lead a band of over 10 talents, Badphayak. Follow us on Instagram @badphayak.
