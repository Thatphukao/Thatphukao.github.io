Summer MAP: Gerrymandering Analysis

My research project focused on quantitatively assessing the effects of district drawing on election results. Using ensemble methods and statistical analysis, we compared valid district maps with real-life scenarios, measuring metrics like partisan fairness and compliance with the Voting Rights Act.

This project combined my computer science skills in algorithm design with my advocacy for fair political systems, applying these to a pressing global issue, including analysis of the political landscape in Thailand.

Data Operations Internship at SpaySecure

At SpaySecure, my role as a Data Operations Intern involved conducting market research and setting up APIs. SpaySecure is dedicated to simplifying sterilization contracts for breeders, ensuring puppy health, and protecting breeders' genetic property.

My contribution supported SpaySecure's mission to provide breeders with the tools to enforce sterilization contracts effectively, thus minimizing backyard breeding and improving puppy health.
